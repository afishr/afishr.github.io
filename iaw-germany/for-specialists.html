for interested engineers and scientists

You may have the opportunity to be placed in a development team by IAW. Please ad here to these conditions (application policy) and use this form for your CV (Curriculum Vitae).

Once we have received your written application, the following steps apply:

  • Check if the applicant meets the entering criteria for the IAW program
  • Interview with IAW staff in the applicant's home country in native language
  • Interview with IAW staff via Skype™ in English
  • Compare and match applicant's skills and expectations with offers / demands in German companies

Subsequently, the following steps apply:

  • Preparatory German courses in applicant's home country, if necessary
  • Determine a time line for the stay in Germany; during the internship in Germany, IAW provides accommodation in fully equipped apartments

As of now, we focus our placements on:

  • Disciplines: IT, electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, structural engineering (statics)
  • Locations: Technology-focused regions: Stuttgart, Rottweil, Tuttlingen, Villingen-Schwenningen, and Donaueschingen in South Western Germany (federal state of Baden-Wuerttemberg)

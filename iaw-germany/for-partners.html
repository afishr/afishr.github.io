opportunities and advantages for our partner companies

We put our partner companies in contact with university graduates with a superior performance profile.

We have the following expectations as regards potential candidates:Applicants must have excellent academic grades and a recommendation by their professors. Additionally, every applicant is assessed individually during the selection process. IAW explicitly favors and supports socially disadvantaged applicants.

It is also possible to assign specifictopics in research and developmentas a Master's thesis or dissertation project.

At this point, we cooperate with 15 mid-tier and large enterprises, active in the sectors of drive technology, automotive, mechanical engineering, medical engineering, measurement techniques, microelectronics, sensor technology, software development, control systems technology, time tracking and recognition systems.

Furthermore, development and researchprojects can be conducted in IAW competence centers in cooperationwith Technical Universities,examples of which include:

  • Embedded systems for microsensor technology
  • Control systems for a variety of applications
  • Systems for simulation and monitoring of energy flow and conversion processes
  • Systems for simulation and optimization of energy consumption

our projects

At IAW, we

Prepare current and graduated students as well as PhD candidates from South Eastern European universities for work positions and internships in German companies
Support engineers and scientists upon return to their home countries and facilitate the formation of development and research teams and founding of start-ups there
Mediate positions in development and research teams as well as internshipsand trainee programs in Germany
Support the establishment of competence centers in Eastern European universities by mediating analysis and research projects offered by the German industry
Offer German language courses, subject-specific seminars, and intercultural workshops
Assist participants during their staying Germany
